# AgriShield 30-Second Pitch

AgriShield turns climate risk into farm actions.

In Central Vietnam, farmers may hear that a typhoon or heavy rain is coming, but the hard question is what to do in the next 24-72 hours for a specific crop stage. Our demo focuses on rice advisory zones in Da Nang and Hue. It combines explainable heavy-rain and typhoon risk scoring with a crop-stage action layer, then generates a short Vietnamese alert that a cooperative or extension officer can send to farmers.

The key claim is deliberately practical: this is not village-level prediction. It is a transparent decision-support platform that helps local teams move from warning to action.
